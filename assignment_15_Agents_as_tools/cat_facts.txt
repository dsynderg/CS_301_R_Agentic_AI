Cat fact 1: Cats sleep about 12–16 hours a day, which works out to roughly two-thirds of their lives.
Cat fact 2: Cats cannot taste sweetness. They lack a functional sweet taste receptor (the T1R2 gene).
Cat fact 3: Cats have a third eyelid, called the nictitating membrane, that helps protect and moisten the eye.
Cat fact 4: A group of domestic cats is called a clowder.
Cat fact 5: Domestic cats sleep about 13 to 16 hours per day on average.
